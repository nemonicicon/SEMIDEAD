  Two problems:

   baked lightmaps, so it contributes zero ambient light. With Lumen off and only a directional light,
  any surface not directly facing the sun is completely black. That explains the black scene.
  2. Units spawn at X=500/X=1500 — the camera focus is at roughly X=172. Units are within the FOV
  geometrically, but given the camera spawns at origin (no PlayerStart in the level until
  CDTestMapManager's BeginPlay runs — which is too late), the exact position is uncertain.
